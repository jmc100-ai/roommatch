v3 — What changed & why

Direct response to "the hierarchy still isn't decisive enough." Every change is aimed at momentum, not just layout.

Room Style: full-height hero card Thumbnail swipe row Momentum banner Story bar: filled chips + pulse Dates: "Commitment moment" copy CTA copy upgrades throughout Chapter-height screens Fade-slide transitions

Room Style — the big fix

  • Now a full-viewport hero card (~60vh), not a grid of equal-weight tiles
  • One card dominates at a time — the featured card is the action
  • Thumbnail row below = navigation tool, not the selection UI itself
  • Style name in Cormorant Garamond at 50px+ — feels editorial
  • CTA reads "Select this vibe →" — direction, not just a checkbox
  • Custom text input is secondary — right-size hierarchy

Momentum cues

  • After neighbourhood: banner "Now, what's your room vibe?" slides up from bottom
  • After style: heading shifts to "Almost there" + dates section advances
  • Chapter-height screens so each step feels like entering a new space
  • Fade-slide animation (translateY 14px → 0) on state transitions
  • Step eyebrow changes per state: "Step 2", "Your differentiator", "Almost there"

Story Bar v3 upgrades

  • Completed steps: filled chip (rgba bg + border)
  • Next-up step: pulsing amber border animation
  • Current step: accent color label
  • Dates = persistent amber CTA pill (right-aligned)
  • Each chip is clickable to jump back and edit
  • Bar appears after city is selected, stays for all steps

Dates — Commitment Moment

  • Heading: "When are you going?" not "Select dates"
  • Card header: "See prices for your nights" — value-focused
  • Primary CTA: "Show rooms with prices →" — implies reward
  • Skip: "Just browsing for now" — lowers resistance vs "Skip"
  • Soft gate copy explains the benefit of adding dates
  • Dates card references the chosen style name (e.g. "Dark & Moody")

Stage contrast / chapter feel

  • Each state uses min-height: calc(100vh - bars) = full-screen moment
  • Eyebrow line (small uppercase) + large Cormorant heading = clear new chapter
  • No elements bleed between steps — no scroll fatigue
  • Room Style step: hero card fills ~60vh before thumbnails appear
  • Results: command bar takes over — mode change is visually obvious

Open questions for v4

  • Multi-select styles: allow 2–3? Would power-blend queries
  • Real room photos as card backgrounds vs gradients?
  • Neighbourhood skip from story bar: 1-tap vs full return to step?
  • Results: should style thumbnails reappear as a horizontal filter strip?
  • Animation: cross-fade between featured style cards (like a slow ken burns)?
  • Dates: calendar picker vs text date fields on desktop?
