FAQ

Common questions

Where should I stay in Mexico City for food and nightlife?

Roma Norte and Condesa — independent restaurants, bars, and terrace culture. Juárez sits between them with strong connectivity.

Where should I stay for museums in CDMX?

Polanco for Museo Soumaya and Chapultepec; Centro for historic core sites. Match your days to the barrio, then pick a room you actually like.

Roma Norte vs Condesa — how do I choose?

Roma skews trendier and louder after dark; Condesa is leafier and calmer. Our comparison page breaks down walkability, price, and vibe.
