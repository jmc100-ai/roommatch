What is the best area to stay near Chapultepec Park?

Polanco is the classic Chapultepec base — Museo Soumaya, leafy avenues, and quick park access. Juárez and Condesa are slightly south with strong café culture.

Which hotels are walking distance to Chapultepec?

Browse our Polanco and Juárez hotel picks — many properties sit within a 10–15 minute walk of the park's eastern edge.

Is Polanco or Condesa better for Chapultepec?

Polanco for museum mile and upscale dining; Condesa for park-adjacent mornings with a leafier, café-led rhythm.
