Who should stay in Shoreditch?

Creative travellers, nightlife seekers, and guests who prefer warehouse boutique moods over royal landmarks.

Who should stay in Westminster?

First-timers who want Big Ben, Westminster Abbey, and postcard London within walking distance.

Our verdict: Shoreditch for East London edge and rooftop bars. Westminster for royal icons and Thames walks. If you will taxi everywhere anyway, Shoreditch's creative energy wins; if you walk all day among landmarks, Westminster is hard to beat.
