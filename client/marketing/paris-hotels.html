FAQ

Common questions

How do I find Paris hotels with real room photos?

TravelByVibe indexes hotel room photography and matches it to plain-language searches — bathrooms, light, layout, and design mood — so you see suites before you book elsewhere.

What is visual hotel search for Paris?

Type the room you picture — Haussmann bedroom, rainfall shower, balcony view — and we rank Paris hotels whose indexed photos look like your description.

Are Paris hotel reviews used in search?

Guest reviews are shown on hotel detail pages when available. Search ranking uses room photos and neighborhood vibe, not review text.

Which Paris arrondissement has the best boutique hotels?

Le Marais, Saint-Germain, and Montmartre have the deepest boutique stock. Browse our boutique hotels guide or search by vibe for design-forward small hotels.

Is TravelByVibe free to use?

Yes — browsing and visual search are free. Add travel dates when you are ready to see live rates, then book through our partner links.
