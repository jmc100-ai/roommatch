Why stay in Saint-Germain-des-Prés?

Café culture without the Marais weekend crush. Strong museum access, polished bistros, and the Paris of novels and black-and-white photos.

Who it's best for: Couples, return visitors, museum mornings, and anyone who wants to live inside a Left Bank novel for a week.
