FAQ

Common questions

What is the best area to stay in Paris for the first time?

Le Marais and the Latin Quarter lead for first-timers: flat, central, and walkable to major sights. Saint-Germain suits travellers who want Left Bank calm over weekend buzz.

Should first-time visitors stay near the Eiffel Tower?

You can, but many first-timers prefer Le Marais or the Latin Quarter for neighbourhood character and dinner options. Search Eiffel-view rooms on our visual search if the tower is a must.

Is Montmartre good for a first Paris trip?

Montmartre is romantic and scenic but hillier and slightly removed from the core. Great for a second half of a trip or couples; Marais/Latin Quarter are easier for sightseeing on foot.
