Who should stay in the Latin Quarter?

Budget-conscious travellers, students-at-heart, and anyone who wants market mornings and river walks.

Who should stay in Saint-Germain?

Couples and return visitors who want polished Left Bank cafés and museum access.

Our verdict: Latin Quarter for value and lively streets. Saint-Germain for polish and calm. Both reward walkers — pick the room aesthetic on TravelByVibe after you pick the arrondissement.
