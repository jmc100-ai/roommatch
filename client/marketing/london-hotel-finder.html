FAQ

Common questions

Where should I stay in London for 3 days?

For a short trip, stay central: Westminster, Covent Garden, or South Kensington keep museums and dinner within walking distance. Our vibe quiz narrows neighborhood and room style in under a minute.

Westminster or Covent Garden — which is better?

Westminster is iconic and park-led; Covent Garden is buzzier and theatre-focused. See our Westminster vs Covent Garden comparison, then search hotels by room photos.

Is Shoreditch too far from central London?

Shoreditch is east of the core but well connected by Tube and Overground. Trade a few extra minutes on the line for street art and rooftop bars.
