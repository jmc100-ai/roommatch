Which Paris neighborhood is best for Eiffel Tower views?

Trocadéro and parts of the 7th arrondissement face the tower; Latin Quarter and Opéra hotels often advertise partial Eiffel glimpses. Search visual keywords like Eiffel view or balcony.

Are hotels near the Eiffel Tower expensive?

The immediate Trocadéro area skews upscale. Latin Quarter and Montmartre can offer view-adjacent stays with more variety — compare on TravelByVibe by room photos, not just price.
