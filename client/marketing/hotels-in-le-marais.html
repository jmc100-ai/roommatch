Why stay in Le Marais?

Medieval lanes meet contemporary galleries. Mornings feel local; evenings spill onto terrace tables. Ideal when you want the Louvre within reach but prefer neighbourhood buzz to grand boulevards.

Who it's best for: First-timers, gallery lovers, foodies, and travellers who picture falafel queues and hidden courtyards over skyline towers.
