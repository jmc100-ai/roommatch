Condesa vs Polanco: Which Mexico City Neighborhood Is Better?

Condesa or Polanco? One is leafy and walkable; the other is polished and luxury-forward. Here is how to choose — and how to find hotels that fit.

Who should stay in Condesa?

First-time visitors, café culture lovers, and anyone who wants jacaranda streets without a car.

Who should stay in Polanco?

Luxury travellers, business guests, museum mornings, and shoppers who want Chapultepec nearby.

Our verdict: Choose Condesa for walkable, leafy days and a younger creative rhythm. Choose Polanco when luxury service, parks, and flagship dining matter more than neighborhood buzz. Either way, match the room on TravelByVibe — a perfect barrio still fails if the suite feels wrong.
