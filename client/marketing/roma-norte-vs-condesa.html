Who should stay in Condesa?

Travellers who want parks, morning runs, and a slightly calmer base — still close to Roma's restaurants.

Who should stay in Roma Norte?

Roma Norte suits food obsessives, nightlife, and return visitors chasing the newest openings.

Our verdict: Stay in Condesa if Parque México mornings matter. Stay in Roma Norte if you are planning dinners first and walks second. Many visitors split the week — pick the hotel vibe that matches how you actually travel.
