How it works

TravelByVibe helps you find a hotel that actually feels right — the neighbourhood, the room, the details you care about — using real photos, not just a single hero shot and a star rating.

1 · Start with your trip

  • Pick a city and who’s travelling.
  • Dates are optional. Browse matches first, or add check-in and check-out anytime to see live nightly rates.

Wizard art from the live app — or swap for one screenshot of the vibe wizard.

2 · Tell us your vibe

  • A short wizard (about a minute) asks how you like to travel: first visit or not, stay style, neighbourhood feel, and must-haves like a balcony or spa-style bath.
  • Your answers are saved for that city. Change them anytime from the bar at the top of your results.

3 · Browse matches ranked for you

  • We compare real room and hotel photos to what you asked for and rank the best fits.
  • Match % is a guide, not a guarantee — use it to spot strong options, then dig into photos and details.
  • Narrow the list with sort, budget, dates, and “available only” when you’re ready.

4 · Book when you’re ready

  • Tap Find & Book to open our partner’s checkout (with your dates when you’ve added them).
  • Always double-check price, cancellation policy, and accessibility on the booking site before you pay.
