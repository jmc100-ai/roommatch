Interaction spec (this prototype)

ComboboxClick or focus the main field → panel opens with all cities listed (no typing required). Filter narrows the list client-side.
Popular chipsOne tap sets the field value and closes the panel. Mexico City & Paris are live in prod today.
Go →Live cities show a success toast; others show a “coming soon” toast (prod would gate with the launch-city modal).
Keyboard↑↓ moves highlight, Enter selects, Escape closes. Typing in the main field also filters.
FooterStatic “✨ More cities coming soon” — signals the curated list will grow.
